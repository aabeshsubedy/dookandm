The problem

Social sellers run a real business inside DMs

Storefront-first tools push you onto a website. Your customers are already messaging you. DokaanDM meets them — and you — there.

01

DMs everywhere, memory nowhere

Messenger, Instagram, and comments in separate tabs. Who ordered what lives in scrollback.

02

Orders only in your head

15–20 active custom orders tracked by memory. No status, no totals, no paper trail.

03

COD returns bleed cash

Return-to-origin can hit ~30% in similar markets — shipping both ways, zero revenue.
